Adoption v. Purebreds

Adoption

Pros

Cons

You are saving their lives by rescuing dogs in shelters because if they are left in shelters for long enough they might get put down.

Mixed breed dogs tend to have less inherited genetic health problems.

The cost of adopting from a shelter is much lower than the cost of purchasing a puppy from a breeder.

If adopting a dog straight from a shelter, you may not know much about how they will act in your home.

Purebreds

Pros

Cons

Genetic health testing to make sure your dog is not likely to carry any inherited genetic problems.

There are millions of dogs dying in shelters each year. Rescuing a dog will save one of those lives.

You know exactly what you are getting in terms of breed—make sure you find the right breed for your family.

Purebreds can be very expensive.

How to take care of your dog...

.... When its's cold outside

  • Don’t walk your dogs in freezing temperatures because it can cause health problems like frostbite, difficulty breathing, and injuries.
  • Do dress them appropriately with coats and boots.

... When it's hot outside

  • Do avoid walking your dog when it is extremely hot outside.
  • Do keep your dog cool indoors and provide them with plenty of water.

... When they're sick

  • Do take them to the vet if the probleml is too serious.
  • Do hold your dog down so it won’t move when you need to give them medicine.
